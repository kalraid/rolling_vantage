# Meta GP buys unlocks, not stat power

Meta GP (the permanent currency carried across Runs) funds new Master/Natial roster unlocks, new Skills, and new Support Skills — it deliberately does not buy permanent stat increases (HP, attack, etc.) or starting-resource bonuses for a Run. The alternative (stat-based meta upgrades) is the default pattern in most roguelites, but was rejected here because Territory count is the sole driver of Dice Pool size (see [[CONTEXT.md]]), and stacking permanent stat power on top of that resource-scaling mechanic risks making early Territory snowballing trivial and erasing the difficulty curve a Run is supposed to provide. Unlocks widen the player's strategic *options* each Chapter without inflating raw power.

## Consequences

Difficulty tuning must come from roster/skill variety and map generation, not from a "the player is just numerically stronger now" lever — there is no stat-based safety valve to lean on if a Chapter turns out too hard for returning players.
